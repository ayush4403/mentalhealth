Mental health tips:
 
1)Just 30 minutes of walking every day can help boost your mood and improve your health. 
2)Good amount of water can improve your energy and focus throughout the day. Also, limit caffeinated beverages such as soft drinks or coffee.
3)Blue light from devices and screens can make it harder to fall asleep, so reduce blue light exposure from your phone or computer before bedtime.
4)Remind yourself daily of things you are grateful for. Be specific. Write them down at night, or replay them in your mind.
5)identify and challenge your negative and unhelpful thoughts.
6)Reach out to your friends or family members who can provide emotional support and practical help.
7)Being in natural surroundings has been shown to have positive effects on mental health.
8)Incorporate mindfulness practices, such as meditation and yoga, into your routine.
9)Make time for hobbies and activities that bring you joy and relaxation.
10)Reading one page per day can help for mental well being.
11)Engaging in mind-boggling games involves a combination of memory, decision-making and strategising, which keeps the brain active and prevents dementia. In addition, playing in a group will boost interaction.
